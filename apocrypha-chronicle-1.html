Chronicle I: Infection Remembers

Seven Forbidden Truths  ·  What the Chronicle Knew But Could Not Show

The Pale Archivum sealed this document seventeen months after Chronicle I was released.
They sealed it after documenting what happened to the first seventeen readers.
None of the seventeen refused to describe what happened to them.
They simply could not. The words they needed did not exist
in any language that had not already been consumed.

← Chronicle I Apocrypha — Chronicle I Lore ↗
The Pale Archivum documented seventeen readers before sealing this document.
All seventeen reported the same experience upon completing it:
the sense that the room they were in had changed dimensions.
Not larger. Not smaller. Wrong.
Like a room they had always known had been quietly rearranged while they were reading.
Twelve of the seventeen reported this sensation persisting for more than a week.
Three reported it has not fully resolved.
The Archivum's notation: "We believe the sensation is accurate, not hallucinatory.
We believe the reader's perception of their environment has been recalibrated.
We do not know what it has been recalibrated toward."
